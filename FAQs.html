FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find answers to common questions about our products, ordering process, and more

How do I place an order?

Ordering from Ape Tides is simple! Just browse our collections, select your items, choose your size, and click "Add to Cart." When you're ready, proceed to checkout where you'll enter your shipping information and payment details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), M-Pesa, and PayPal. All transactions are securely processed through our encrypted payment gateway.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

We process orders quickly to ensure fast delivery. If you need to modify or cancel your order, please contact us immediately at orders@apetides.com with your order number. We'll do our best to accommodate your request if your order hasn't been processed for shipping yet.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary depending on your location and the shipping method selected. For domestic orders within Kenya, standard shipping takes 3-5 business days, while express shipping takes 1-2 business days. International shipping times vary by destination (typically 7-21 business days).

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes! We ship worldwide to most countries. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. During checkout, you'll see the available shipping options and costs for your location.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package through our website or directly with the carrier.

What is your return policy?

We offer returns within 14 days of delivery for unworn, unwashed items with original tags attached. Sale items are final sale and cannot be returned. Please visit our Returns & Exchanges page for complete details.

How do I initiate a return?

To initiate a return, please email returns@apetides.com with your order number and the items you wish to return. We'll provide you with return instructions and a return authorization number.

How long does it take to process a refund?

Once we receive your returned items, please allow 3-5 business days for inspection and processing. Refunds will be issued to your original payment method. Depending on your bank, it may take additional time for the refund to appear on your statement.

How do I choose the right size?

We provide detailed size charts for each product on the product page. Measure yourself and compare with our size chart for the best fit. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a looser fit or down for a more fitted look.

Are your products true to size?

Our products are generally true to size based on standard measurements. However, some items may have an oversized or fitted cut, which will be noted in the product description. We recommend checking the specific size chart for each item.

How should I care for my Ape Tides clothing?

For best results, machine wash cold with similar colors and tumble dry low. Avoid bleach and ironing directly on prints. Specific care instructions are included with each garment on a care label.

Do you restock sold out items?

We occasionally restock popular items, but many of our designs are limited edition. If an item you want is sold out, you can sign up for restock notifications on the product page.

STILL HAVE QUESTIONS?

Our customer service team is happy to help with any additional questions you may have.
