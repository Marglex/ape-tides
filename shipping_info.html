SHIPPING INFORMATION

Everything you need to know about our shipping policies and delivery options

DOMESTIC SHIPPING

We offer fast and reliable shipping throughout Kenya with our trusted delivery partners.

Service Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping 3-5 business days KES 300
Express Shipping 1-2 business days KES 600
Nairobi Same-Day Delivery Same day (order before 12pm) KES 800

Note: Delivery times are estimates and may vary depending on your location and other factors beyond our control.

INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING

We ship worldwide to bring Ape Tides streetwear to our global community.

Region Delivery Time Cost (Starting From)
East Africa 5-7 business days KES 1,200
Rest of Africa 7-14 business days KES 2,500
Europe 10-15 business days KES 3,800
North America 12-18 business days KES 4,200
Asia & Oceania 14-21 business days KES 4,500

International customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or import fees that may apply to their order.

ORDER PROCESSING

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and public holidays).

You will receive a confirmation email with your order details and a tracking number once your order has shipped.

TRACKING YOUR ORDER

Once your order has been shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package through our website or directly with the carrier.

SHIPPING RESTRICTIONS

We currently do not ship to the following countries:

  • North Korea
  • Syria
  • Crimea Region

If your country is not listed above but you're having trouble placing an order, please contact our customer service team.

MORE QUESTIONS?

If you have any questions about shipping that weren't answered here, please don't hesitate to contact our customer service team at shipping@apetides.com or through our contact page.
