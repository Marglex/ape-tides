RETURNS & EXCHANGES

OUR RETURN POLICY

We want you to be completely satisfied with your Ape Tides purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we're happy to help with returns or exchanges within 30 days of delivery.

RETURNS

  • 30-day return policy
  • Items must be unworn and unwashed
  • Original tags must be attached
  • Original packaging preferred

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 5-7 business days after we receive your return.

EXCHANGES

  • Free size exchanges within 30 days
  • Subject to availability
  • Original condition required
  • Color/style exchanges treated as returns

If the item you want is out of stock, we'll process a refund instead.

NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS

  • Final sale items (marked as such)
  • Worn or washed items
  • Items without original tags
  • Personalized/custom items

Swimwear and underwear must have hygiene seals intact to be eligible for return.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to our error (wrong item shipped, defective item, etc.). We recommend using a trackable shipping method as we cannot be responsible for lost return packages.

HOW TO RETURN OR EXCHANGE

Follow these simple steps to return or exchange your Ape Tides purchase:

1

Initiate Your Return

Start your return process by filling out our online return form below. You'll receive a return authorization number and instructions via email.

Alternatively, you can email us at returns@apetides.com with your order number and the items you'd like to return.

2

Package Your Items

Securely package your return items in the original packaging if possible. Include:

  • The items you're returning
  • Original tags attached
  • Completed return form (printed from email)

We recommend using a sturdy box to prevent damage during transit.

3

Ship Your Package

Ship your return package to our fulfillment center at the following address:

Ape Tides Returns
123 Urban Way
Brooklyn, NY 11201
United States

Remember to keep your tracking number until your return is processed.

4

Receive Your Refund or Exchange

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund or exchange within 3-5 business days. You'll receive an email confirmation when your return is complete.

Refunds typically appear in your account within 5-10 business days depending on your financial institution.
