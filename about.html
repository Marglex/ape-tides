REDEFINING STREETWEAR

Founded in 2018, Ape Tides emerged from a shared passion for urban culture and premium apparel. What began as a small project between friends in a Brooklyn studio has grown into a respected name in contemporary streetwear.

Our name reflects our philosophy - the primal energy of urban life (Ape) meeting the constant flow and change of fashion (Tides). We believe in clothing that makes a statement while standing the test of time.

Every piece in our collections is designed with meticulous attention to detail, using only the finest materials sourced through ethical and sustainable practices. We work with skilled artisans who share our commitment to quality and craftsmanship.

OUR MISSION

To create streetwear that blends bold design with uncompromising quality, empowering individuals to express their authentic selves through clothing that feels as good as it looks.

Quality First

We never compromise on materials or construction. Every stitch, seam, and fabric choice is carefully considered for durability and comfort.

Sustainable Practices

From organic cottons to recycled materials, we're committed to reducing our environmental impact at every stage of production.

Community Focused

We support emerging artists and collaborate with local creatives to bring fresh perspectives to our designs.

Innovative Design

Pushing boundaries while respecting streetwear roots, we create pieces that feel both fresh and timeless.
